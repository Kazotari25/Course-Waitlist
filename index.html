About your Instructor

Hi! I'm kazotarimae. A self-taught digital artist who loves creating soft, dreamy anime characters. I remember the frustration of knowing what I wanted to create but lacking the technical foundation to bring my vision to life. The countless hours spent wondering if I was practicing the right things, if my art would ever be "good enough," and whether making a living as an artist was even possible. Through years of dedicated practice, professional work, and real-world experience, I've developed a systematic approach that eliminates guesswork and accelerates artistic growth.

This isn't just another drawing tutorial series. This is the complete roadmap I wish I had when I started – a comprehensive system that takes you from fundamental skills to professional-level work, while helping you develop your unique artistic voice.
